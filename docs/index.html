One prompt in. A whole world, written out.

Auto-Writer turns a single prompt into a complete story. From your idea it crafts the world, the dialogue, the vibrant environment, and the story itself. The story is written in the app and saved to your library — organized in folders, editable any time, and kept in your browser rather than the repository.

🌍 World

Geography, history, factions, and lore, built to stay consistent.

💬 Dialogue

Natural, distinct voices for every character.

🎨 Environment

Vivid, sensory scenes you can picture.

📖 Story

A full arc from opening to resolution.

How it writes

Stories are generated in 2,000-word chunks, each continuing seamlessly from the last so world facts, character voices, and plot threads stay consistent across any length. All output is written in English.

Your input

Your prompt can be anything from a single sentence to a detailed brief of up to 10,000 words. More context means a closer match to your vision.

Your stories stay in your library

Stories are created and written in the app, and they live in your library — saved in your browser, never uploaded to the repository. Organize them into folders, and rename, move, edit, or delete any story at any time. Only the app's own code is version-controlled on GitHub; your writing is yours and stays local. Use Backup in the library to download a copy you can keep or restore later.
